Who's Dave

I am learner to my core and love to share whatever knowledge I pick up. I’m passionate about people. It gives me great joy to advocate for individuals and see them grow in whatever skills their striving for. To be apart of somebody else’s growth is special.

I try to embrace discomfort because it leads to growth (hence Ultrarunning, taking cold showers). Nols taught me the importance of having TAU (Tolerance for Adversity and Uncertainty) and it’s a game changer!
